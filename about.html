A Reflection on Lookism

A Message from the Creator

The suggestions made to change users' faces are interpretations of face readings. Actual face fortune telling requires years of studying and training. Thus, what has been said should be treated lightly.

More importantly, I would like to emphasize that this project was by no means meant to critique plastic surgery or one's appearance. Rather, I hope to make people who interact with A Reflection on Lookism reflect upon how society places importance on looks.

For more information on the context and inspiration behind my capstone, please refer to my project statement or informational pamphlet below.
