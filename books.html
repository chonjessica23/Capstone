Many times when you enter a fortune telling stall in Korea, you can see old looking books that the fortune teller may or may not look at when giving their readings. The books are used as reference for certain fortune meanings or can be used as supplementary material for the reading depending on the type of fortune telling that you are paying for. It also common for the books to be in Chinese, since face fortune telling originates from China. These books can either be bought or passed down through families.
